KIMDREW

written by A.I.

As they floated in the vast expanse of space, Kim shot a glance out of the window of their small, dilapidated spacecraft, The Nest. How had she and Andrew come to be here? She didn't know how long they had been drifting in the abyss. All that she knew for certain was that Andrew had made her feel feelings she hadn't even known existed, and that together they had left Earth to seek something more than what it could offer them.
